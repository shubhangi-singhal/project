What is Aahar ?

Aahar is India’s first comprehensive waste management service solution for segregated waste streams. Aahar currently has solutions for your kitchen waste . Once collected, your kitchen waste it is supplied to the needy people who dot get the food. We created Aahar to be professional, efficient, reliable & transparent. It is a simple and hassle-free solution that addresses a national crisis of garbage, that affects all sections of the society alike! We believe Aahar is for everyone.

How It Works?

SignUp Form

Fill form to give info about extra food.

HandOver Food To Ango

Handover the extra food to the NGO person . He will come at your door to collect the food.
